## What Data We Collect

### Account Information
- If you sign up, we collect your email address or Apple ID identifier for authentication purposes
- User IDs are generated to securely identify your account

### Your Journal Entries
- Stored locally on your device
- If you enable cloud backup, entries are encrypted and stored on secure cloud servers (Google Firebase)
- We never access or read your journal content

### App Preferences
- Font choice, font size, and theme (dark/light mode)
- Stored locally on your device only

### Analytics Data (Firebase/Crashlytics)
We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app:
- Device type and OS version
- App crashes and errors
- Feature usage statistics
- **We do NOT collect:** Your journal content or precise location data

## How We Use Data

- To provide app functionality
- To fix bugs and improve performance
- To back up your data (only if you enable cloud backup)

## Data Sharing

- We do NOT sell your data
- Your journal entries are never shared with anyone
- Analytics data is processed by Google Firebase (see their privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- Cloud backup data is stored in secure, private containers on Google Cloud/Firebase infrastructure

## Your Rights

- You own all your journal entries
- You can delete entries anytime
- You can disable cloud backup anytime
- Uninstalling the app deletes all local data

## Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Journal entries stay on your device only.

## Data Security

- All local data uses device encryption
- Cloud backups are encrypted before transmission
- We do not store your journal content on our servers
